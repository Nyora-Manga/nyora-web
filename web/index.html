Nyora — Free Online Manga Reader in Your Browser

Nyora is a free online manga reader that runs entirely in your browser — no download, no sign-up, no install. Open a tab and start reading manga from hundreds of sources parsed client-side, with a standard and webtoon reader supporting left-to-right, right-to-left and continuous vertical modes.

Read manga online, free, with no install

Keep favourites and reading history, track series with AniList, and sync your library to the cloud with Google. Everything runs locally in your browser, so there is nothing to install and nothing to set up.

An installable, offline-capable PWA

Install Nyora as a PWA for an app-like, offline-capable experience, or just use it in the browser. Nyora is cross-platform — also available on Android, Windows, macOS, Linux and iOS.

  • Installable PWA with an offline-capable app shell
  • Hundreds of sources parsed client-side
  • Standard & webtoon reader — LTR, RTL and vertical
  • Favourites and reading history
